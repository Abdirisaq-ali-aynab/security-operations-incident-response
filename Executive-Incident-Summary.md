# Executive Incident Summary

## Incident Overview
A high-severity security alert identified suspicious activity on a Windows workstation, indicating a potential malware infection. The alert was generated through security monitoring controls and required immediate investigation to assess scope and impact.

---

## Business Risk
If left uncontained, the incident posed a risk of credential exposure, unauthorized internal access, lateral movement, and potential disruption to business operations. Prompt response was required to reduce security and operational impact.

---

## Actions Taken
- Validated the security alert and confirmed indicators of compromise  
- Investigated the affected endpoint to identify malicious activity and persistence mechanisms  
- Recommended isolation of the impacted system to prevent further spread  
- Advised credential hygiene actions, including password resets where appropriate  
- Documented remediation steps and validated system integrity post-remediation  

---

## Recommendations
- Enhance endpoint monitoring and detection coverage  
- Strengthen credential management and access control practices  
- Maintain documented and repeatable incident response procedures  
- Continue proactive monitoring for related indicators to prevent recurrence
